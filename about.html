How It Began

Built on Experience, Driven by Vision

Circle Outsourcing Solutions began with a simple idea sparked during the pandemic. A group of experienced professionals came together to reflect on the changing business landscape and recognized the growing demand for outsourcing solutions.

With years of industry expertise and a shared vision, they decided to turn that idea into reality. What started as a conversation soon became Circle Outsourcing Solutions—a company dedicated to helping businesses grow through reliable, scalable, and people-focused services.

Our Mission

To deliver scalable outsourcing solutions that meet evolving business needs and drive value for our clients, while fostering positive community impact and sustainable growth through meaningful service and social responsibility.

Our Vision

To be a trusted outsourcing partner recognized for creating customized, people-centered solutions that transform businesses, empower communities, and make a lasting positive impact in the world.

Our Approach

Talent Acquisition Capabilities

Circle Outsourcing Solutions recognizes that our success hinges on the quality of our workforce, and our Talent Acquisition strategy reflects that priority — built around finding and securing the people who help our clients build high-performing teams.

Our Talent Acquisition practices begin with a comprehensive understanding of each client's needs and requirements. From there, we attract top talent through compelling branding initiatives and by leveraging the right channels, engaging both active and passive candidates through our Professional Network, LGUs, and academic partners. This gives our clients access to a diverse range of candidates while positioning Circle Outsourcing Solutions as an employer of choice.
